Hello !! we are Coffeeforall shop & gallery We all used to have almost the same background as a bachelor student but in different countries. Now we are master’s student at the same school. apart from school, we show Praha citizens and foreign visitors through prague by encouraging them to have to our test coffee. We have started Coffeeforall shop & gallery in this year. We wanted to chat with different people from abroad and our citizen themselves, having our coffee with them, and, with any luck. We were all born out of Europe but we like prague. We adore the city and we think it’s contagious. our coffee is fair the beginning point of what we do: make beyond any doubt you have got an awesome time in Prague. Coffeeforall shop & gallery offers you a unique cozy warm coffee house environment unlike any other in Prague. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee although you are more than welcome to do that. We are a place where you can sit down and enjoy your coffee. If you want to work, we have created a seating specially for the comfort of the people that would like to spend some time working while sipping their cup of coffee. If you want to rest/ relax, we have created a cozy soft- seating corner in front of a beautifully designed architecture of coffee origin.

We offer a delicious variety of coffee from Europe and Africa. And you can complete your coffee with our delicious sweet treats by our own baker. We are looking forward to serving you at coffeeforall shop and gallery.Coffeeforall shop & gallery, we are your neighborhood coffee shop with a flair for the eclectic. As the longest running independent coffee shop in Praha, we believe in creating an atmosphere that focuses on making friends, drinking good coffee from organic fair trade single origin farms, and eating vegetarian and vegan foods made from scratch. If you are looking for a place to call your own, look no further - come hang out, please we are here for you.
